About us

Alinova is a company founded on January 1st of 2012. Since then, the company's direction has shifted greatly towards the web, but its focus has remained the same. Alinova's mission is to achieve beautiful and unique user experience in web apps and at the operating system level.

Our Projects

Some of our projects have been based extensively around HTML5 and the new capabilities it has brought to the web. Because the web is such an innovative and complex area, Alinova has devoted an entire branch towards developing for it. Find out more about this by visiting our Techboxcoding branch.
